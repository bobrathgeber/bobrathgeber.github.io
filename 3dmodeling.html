Copper Axehead

A copper axehead featuring two birds I modeled for use in the game Digology. This object can be found at the Peru digsite.

Fossilized Fish

One of my first 3D Renders of a fossilized fish used in the game Digology.

Headdress of the Sea God

This is a solid gold Incan artifact found in Peru. I recreated this object as a 3D model to use at the Peruvian digsite for the game Digology.

Pterosaur Skeleton

Another item I created for Digology. This model of a prepared pterosaur skeleton. In Digology, the player would find pieces of this skeleton and have to assemble them to create this in full.

Shark with Saddle

The shark with a saddle is one of the unlockable ships available in River of Gold. The protagonist, Sir Lad, rides this shark down the river to fight obstacles while he collects treasure.

Shop Keeper

This is the shop keeper you encounter while playing River of Gold. He has animations where he paddles into the scene, shows off his goods, and jumps for joy when the player purchases an item.

Sir Lad the Explorer

Nicknamed as "Sir Lad" early on, this is the character you play as in River of Gold. He is an archaelogist who accidently blew up a pyramid with lots of treasures. He is sailing down the Nile trying to collect as much as he can without sinking.

Incan Artifact Clay Trumpet

A clay trumpet used in the game Digology. It is one of the many artifacts that can be dug up during an excavation.
